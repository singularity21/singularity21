Concepts in 3 levels

Ever had a “Eureka” moment when you approached a subject from a fresh and simpler perspective, completely different from what you learnt from the lecture or the book? Think you have a profound understanding of a topic? Look no further, we have the perfect challenge for you. Buckle up to demystify concepts and phenomena that might seem puzzling and hard to grasp at first glance. The catch? Work your way through explaining them in three levels of complexity; to an elementary schooler, an undergrad, and a Ph.D. scholar. A fun to watch and demanding to participate contest!
rules
a. Students can prepare a presentation on any topic they like.
b. They must record giving the presentation (like a talk) and upload the
recording on Google Drive and share an accessible link in the form provided.
c. The recording may or may not include the participant’s video. It is up to the participant.
d. Participants can record their presentations using Google Meet or any other software they prefer.
e. It is recommended for participants to spend 1.5 minutes per level and 5 minutes overall. Any entries longer than 6 minutes will be ignored.
