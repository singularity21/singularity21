Crypto Roulette

Want to explore and leverage the rapidly growing Cryptocurrency Industry, as well as experience the volatility of this FUD-related market without risking your own money? Congratulations! We'd like to invite you to participate in Singularity'21 *Crypto Roulette*, a mock Crypto Trading Competition.
Through this competition, the IISER- Bhopal Economics Club aims to inculcate the knowledge and experience of the cryptocurrency market with real-world trading without actually investing their own cash.
