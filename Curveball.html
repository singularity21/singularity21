Curveball

"IICE-CurveBall" is back this Singularity 2021 in association with the Innovation and Incubation Centre for Entrepreneurship (IICE), IISER Bhopal. CurveBall is an institute-level entrepreneurial competition, flagship event of IICE-IISER Bhopal, and the salient component of Singularity.
The competition provides an excellent platform to present innovative business ideas and to test the market potential of the same. Showcase your inner entrepreneur and pitch your idea to a panel of judges with substantial experience: the best, but also the most unyielding jury you could have asked for!
Prizes:
Winner: Rs. 5,000
First Runner-up: Rs. 3,000
Second Runner-up: Rs. 2,000
