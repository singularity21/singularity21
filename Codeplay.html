Code Play

Hello peeps of Natural Intelligence! Come join us at our very own mini-hackathon at CodePlay, Singularity’21. Team up strategically with your fellow geeks as the concepts are interdisciplinary.You can participate in terrific trios, dynamic duos or lone-wolves.
rules
1) Event is fully online.
2)) Any programming language is allowed. If using a scripting language, then give instructions to compile it. Better algorithm will be the tie-breaker for higher rank.
3) Multiple systems can be used to solve questions but single submission per team is allowed which would have all required codes and answers.
4) Duration: 1 hour (+ 15 mins grace).
Prizes worth 3.5K!
PS: People from all departments are very welcome as this is not only a coding dependent event. Look forward to seeing puzzles and riddles too!!!
