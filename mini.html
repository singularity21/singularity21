Mini-iGem

Team iGEM IISER Bhopal is proud to present an exciting opportunity for you to get into the depths and get a feel of what the iGEM Competition is all about.
Make teams of up to five or go solo and come up with a detailed proposal to solve one of the problems given, in an iGEM-esque way and win prizes. Use Genetic Engineering to solve the problem combining both your synthetic biology as well as engineering skills to come up with innovative solutions to the problem. Shortlisted teams will have to defend their proposals in front of a panel of judges.
Prizes worth 5K!!
