pictionary

Sketch it Quick! Guess it Back! Time to scribble and sketch, Time to hit your brain with wild guesses cuz it’s time for Pictionary !! Pictionary is a super simple game in which the players attempt to identify a word/phenomenon/ from a picture drawn by another player. Let's step into the dictionary of pictures - Pictionary! Super fun game!
Registered participants will be mailed the ZOOM link for the Round-1 of Pictionary. Only those selected in the Round-1 are eligible to participate in Round-2 of Pictionary.
NOTE: This is an Individual event.
Prizes worth 1.5K!
